Mattupetty dam and Swiss farm

Located in the quaint village of Mattupetty, the Indo-Swiss Farm is a dairy farm dedicated to cattle breeding and rearing. Also known as Madupetty, this village is often called ‘The Switzerland of Kerala’ alluding to the Indo-Swiss Livestock Project which resulted in the formation of this farm.
The cattle heads themselves add up to 400! Apart from the animal pastures, there are herbal plants and coco leaves that are planted across the fields. The dairy farm is open to public visit on all days of the week from 9 AM to 11 AM and 2 PM to 3:30 PM. A nominal fee of INR 5 is charged per head.
Mattupetty Dam: This concrete gravity dam overlooks the beautiful Mattupetty Lake. Get here between 9:30 AM and 5 PM to enjoy various types of boating on the lake. You can also go on a horse-back ride on the banks. The top of Mattupetty Dam, accessible to pedestrians, is a nice vantage point for taking pictures.
