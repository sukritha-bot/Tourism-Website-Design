Ramakalmedu

Ramakkalmedu is a hill station and a hamlet in Idukki district in the Indian state of Kerala. The place is noted for its panoramic beauty and numerous windmills. It is located about 15 km from Nedumkandam on the Munnar-Thekkady route. Nearby towns are Thookkupalam (5 km), Kattappana (25 km), and Kumily (40 km).
Ramakalmedu stands tall in the western ghats at a height of 3,500 ft (1,100 m) above sea level.The ecosystem of the area consists largely of grass land Shola forest type which is laced by sporadic bamboo forests.Constant wind is another factor which makes Ramakkalmedu unique.
Wind blows at a speed around 35 km/ hour at Ramakkalmedu throughout the year irrespective of the season and time.
